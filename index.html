Base station

Sign in to ArcGIS

Enter your ArcGIS Online username and password. Survey123 to MapMaker exchanges them for a short-lived token and acts as you.

Built-in accounts only. Username/password sign-in works for ArcGIS built-in accounts. Organizational single sign-on (SSO/SAML) and social logins can't issue a token this way and won't work here. Your credentials are used once to get a token and are never stored.
Step 01 — Survey

Which survey, and who should see it?

Choose the Survey123 survey to publish. This list shows the hosted results layers (feature services ending in _results) in your account, most recent first.

Public sharing exposes the underlying survey records to anyone. Confirm your data contains no personal or sensitive information before continuing.
